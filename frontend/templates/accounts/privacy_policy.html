Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 2026 · Compliant with nDSG (Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection) and GDPR

1. Who We Are

This platform is operated by Powercoders, a non-profit association based in Switzerland. We run ICT integration programmes for people from refugee and migrant backgrounds. This platform — the Powercoders Learning Journey Hub — is used exclusively by programme participants and teaching staff.

2. What Data We Collect

  • Email address (used as your login identifier)
  • Display name (the name shown on the platform — not your legal name)
  • Optional profile photo (avatar)
  • Learning content you create: journal entries, goals, reflections, tasks, and comments
  • Login timestamps and access logs (for security purposes)

We do not collect your legal name, home address, phone number, or any unnecessary personal data.

3. Why We Collect It

  • To provide you with access to the learning platform
  • To allow teaching staff to support your learning journey
  • To track programme progress for reporting purposes
  • To ensure the security and integrity of the platform

4. Who Can See Your Data

  • You — full access to your own data at all times
  • Your teacher — can see journal entries and tasks you choose to share, your goals, and your weekly reflections
  • Administrators — can see all data for platform management purposes
  • Private journal entries are never visible to teachers or administrators

5. Your Rights (nDSG & GDPR)

  • Right to access — you can download all your personal data at any time from your profile page
  • Right to correction — you can update your display name and profile at any time
  • Right to deletion — you can request full account and data deletion from your profile page
  • Right to object — contact us at any time if you have concerns about how your data is used

6. Data Security

All passwords are hashed using Argon2, the current gold standard in password security. Access to the platform is protected by optional two-factor authentication. All data is stored on servers located in the EU/Switzerland. We notify the FDPIC (Swiss data protection authority) within 72 hours in the event of a data breach.

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained for the duration of your programme participation. After programme completion, data is retained for a maximum of 12 months unless you request earlier deletion.

8. Contact

For any questions or requests regarding your data, contact the Powercoders teaching team or email info@powercoders.org.

By clicking "I Accept", you confirm that you have read and understood this privacy policy.

This acceptance is recorded with a timestamp as required by Swiss law (nDSG).
