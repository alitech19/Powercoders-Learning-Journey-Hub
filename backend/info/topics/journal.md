## Journal list {#list}

Learning journal entries by date. **Private** entries are only for you; **shared** entries are visible to teachers in your groups.

## Entry detail {#detail}

Full entry text, mood, tags, and date.

## Create entry {#form-create}

### Title (required)

Headline in the list and on the detail page.

### Entry date

Which day the entry is **about** (can be in the past). Sorting uses this date, not only when you clicked save.

### Content (required)

Main journal text — what you learned, tried, or reflected on.

### Mood (optional)

Quick emoji scale: Great, Good, Okay, Struggling, Tough — shown in lists for a glance at how you felt that day.

### Tags (optional)

Comma-separated keywords for your own search later (e.g. `react, pair-programming`).

### Visibility

- **Only me** — teachers never see this entry.
- **Share with teachers** — appears in the teacher’s student journal view for your group.

## Edit entry {#form-edit}

Any field can change. Visibility change applies going forward.

## Delete entry {#form-delete}

Permanent deletion.

## For students {#for-students}

The dashboard shows your three most recent entries. Share selectively if you want teacher support.
