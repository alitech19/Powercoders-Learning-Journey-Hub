## Reflections list {#list}

Structured check-ins: **weekly**, **project**, or **custom** tags. Wellbeing questions appear when you complete the final reflection section.

## Reflection detail {#detail}

Expectations at the start and final reflection when you finish. Teachers see **shared** reflections only.

## Create reflection {#form-create}

The form is one long page you can save once and edit later. Fields appear in this order:

### Title (required)

Short label for this check-in, e.g. “Week 12” or “Project Alpha retrospective”. Shown in your list and on the detail page.

### Tags

Optional checkboxes — you can combine them:

- **Weekly** — marks this as a weekly reflection. The dashboard “reflection due” reminder looks for a weekly-tagged reflection with a completed **final reflection** in the current week.
- **Project** — use when the check-in is tied to a project phase or deliverable.
- **Custom** — shows an extra text field **Custom tag label** (e.g. “Midterm”, “Sprint 3”). Only stored when Custom is checked.

### Expectations and plans

Free text at the **start** of the period. Describe what you expect to learn or do. You can edit anytime; the date of last meaningful edit may appear on the detail page.

The form may start with placeholder template text — replace it with your own words. Empty or template-only text does not count as “started” for progress tracking.

### Final reflection

Written when you **wrap up** the period. Same template behaviour as expectations: placeholder text until you write real content.

**Important:** The **Wellbeing check-in** block stays hidden until you have written something real in final reflection (not just the default template). Then six rows appear (see below).

### Wellbeing check-in (appears after final reflection has content)

Each row is optional. Tap one mood per row (Great → Tough) or **Clear**:

| Row on the form | What it asks |
|-----------------|--------------|
| **Energy** | How energetic do you feel? |
| **Calmness** | How calm do you feel? |
| **Engagement** | How engaged do you feel? |
| **Concentration** | How focused do you feel? |
| **Sleep** | How well did you sleep? |
| **Physical activity** | How active do you feel? |

These are snapshots for you and (if shared) your teacher — not medical records.

### Visibility

Two buttons at the bottom — only one active at a time:

- **Only me** — teachers cannot open this reflection in the app.
- **Share with teachers** — supervising teachers for your group can read expectations, final reflection, and wellbeing answers.

You can change visibility when editing; it does not notify anyone automatically.

## Edit reflection {#form-edit}

Same fields as create. Turning on **Share with teachers** later exposes content teachers could not see before.

## Delete reflection {#form-delete}

Permanently removes this check-in.

## For students {#for-students}

Use **Weekly** on at least one reflection per week and complete **final reflection** to clear the dashboard banner. Fill expectations early, final reflection when you finish the week.
